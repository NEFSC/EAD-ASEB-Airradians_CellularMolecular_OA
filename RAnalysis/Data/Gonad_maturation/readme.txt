### Gonad_maturation data folder

**About**: photos and initial visual assessments were completed by several assistants (Katie M. Meghana, Gabby P., Diane, HS interns, etc.) after hemolymph and tissue samples were taken
from 90 F2 Adult Bay scallops - 45 on Day 1 of the experiment (5/2/2023) and 45 on timepoint Day 14 of the experiment (5/26/2023). 

- photos folder = contains all images taken by Katie McFarland

- 'gonad'.csv files = these contain the sample metadata and broad information regarding the gonad status
